Assistant Lecturer | Pharmacist | Nutritionist
----------------------------------------------

I’m a pharmacist and Assistant Lecturer at Misr International University with a Master's degree in Pharmaceutical-Medicinal Chemistry. My passion lies in bridging science with everyday health by combining pharmaceutical knowledge, research, and nutrition education.

Alongside teaching undergraduate pharmacy students and mentoring research projects, I’m expanding my career into nutrition and healthy lifestyle coaching. I enjoy creating evidence-based health content, empowering people to make sustainable lifestyle changes, and helping them improve their overall well-being through practical nutrition and wellness strategies
